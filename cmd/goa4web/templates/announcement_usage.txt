Usage:
  {{.Prog}} announcement <command> [<args>]

Manage site announcements tied to news posts.

Commands:
  list     list announcements
  add      promote a news post to an announcement
  delete   delete an announcement

Examples:
  {{.Prog}} announcement list
  {{.Prog}} announcement add -news-id 42
  {{.Prog}} announcement delete -id 5
