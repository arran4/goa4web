Usage:
  goa4web email send -to <recipient> -subject <subject> -body <body>

The send command sends a test email to the specified recipient. This is useful for verifying that your email configuration is working correctly.

Arguments:
  -to          The recipient's email address.
  -subject     The subject of the email.
  -body        The body of the email.

Examples:
  # Send a test email
  goa4web email send -to "test@example.com" -subject "Test" -body "This is a test email."
