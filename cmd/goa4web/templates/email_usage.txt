Usage:
  {{.Prog}} email <command> [<args>]

The email command is used to manage email-related tasks, such as sending test
emails, managing the email queue, and viewing the email log. This is the
primary interface for all email-related administrative tasks.

Commands:
  failed   Manage failed emails.
  send     Send a test email.
  queue    Manage the queue of emails waiting to be sent.
  sent     Inspect and retry sent emails.
  test     Send a test email to check the email configuration.

Examples:
  # Send a test email
  {{.Prog}} email send -to "test@example.com" -subject "Test" -body "This is a test email."

  # List all emails in the queue
  {{.Prog}} email queue list

  # List failed emails
  {{.Prog}} email failed list

  # List sent emails
  {{.Prog}} email sent list
