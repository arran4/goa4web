Usage:
  {{.Prog}} imagebbs moderation bulk-approve <id> [<id>...]
  {{.Prog}} imagebbs moderation bulk-approve --from-file <path>
  {{.Prog}} imagebbs moderation bulk-approve --from-file -

Description:
  Approve multiple ImageBBS posts by ID. IDs can be provided as positional
  arguments or read from a file (one per line or comma-separated).

Examples:
  {{.Prog}} imagebbs moderation bulk-approve 10 11 12
  {{.Prog}} imagebbs moderation bulk-approve --from-file pending_ids.txt
  cat pending_ids.txt | {{.Prog}} imagebbs moderation bulk-approve --from-file -
