Usage:
  {{.Prog}} imagebbs moderation <command> [<args>]

Description:
  Moderate ImageBBS posts.

Commands:
  approve       Approve a single image post.
  reject        Reject (delete) a single image post.
  bulk-approve  Approve multiple image posts.

Examples:
  {{.Prog}} imagebbs moderation approve 42
  {{.Prog}} imagebbs moderation reject --id 42
  {{.Prog}} imagebbs moderation bulk-approve 10 11 12
  {{.Prog}} imagebbs moderation bulk-approve --from-file pending_ids.txt
