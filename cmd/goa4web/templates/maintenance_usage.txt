Usage:
  {{.Prog}} maintenance <command> [<args>]

The maintenance command runs one-off operational tasks that mirror the admin
maintenance actions.

Commands:
  forum-topic-convert-private  Convert public forum topics to the private handler.

Examples:
  # Convert a single forum topic to private
  {{.Prog}} maintenance forum-topic-convert-private 42

  # Convert multiple forum topics to private
  {{.Prog}} maintenance forum-topic-convert-private 42 84 126
