Usage: {{.Prog}} role grants export -role <role-name> [-format json|csv]

Flags:
  -role    The role name to export.
  -format  Output format. Options: json or csv (default: json).

Examples:
  # Export grants for the "user" role in JSON
  {{.Prog}} role grants export -role user -format json

  # Export grants for the "moderator" role in CSV
  {{.Prog}} role grants export -role moderator -format csv
