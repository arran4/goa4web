What is a4code?
===
[b]a4code[/b] is a lightweight markup language used on this site for formatting text. It uses square brackets to denote formatting tags.

Here are the available tags:

[b]Bold Text[/b]
Usage: [code][b]Bold Text[/b][/code]
Result: [b]Bold Text[/b]

[i]Italic Text[/i]
Usage: [code][i]Italic Text[/i][/code]
Result: [i]Italic Text[/i]

[u]Underlined Text[/u]
Usage: [code][u]Underlined Text[/u][/code]
Result: [u]Underlined Text[/u]

[s]Subscript[/s] and [p]Superscript[/p]
Usage: [code][s]Subscript[/s] and [p]Superscript[/p][/code]
Result: [s]Subscript[/s] and [p]Superscript[/p]

[b]Links[/b]
Usage: [code][link https://example.com Example Website][/code]
Result: [link https://example.com Example Website]
(Note: You can also use [code][url][/code] or [code][a][/code])

[b]Images[/b]
Usage: [code][img https://example.com/image.png][/code]
Result: (Displays the image)

[b]Quotes[/b]
Usage: [code][quote]This is a quote[/quote][/code]
Result:
[quote]This is a quote[/quote]

[b]Quote with Author[/b]
Usage: [code][quoteof User]This is a quote by User[/quoteof][/code]
Result:
[quoteof User]This is a quote by User[/quoteof]

[b]Spoilers[/b]
Usage: [code][spoiler]Hidden text[/spoiler][/code]
Result: [spoiler]Hidden text[/spoiler]

[b]Code Blocks[/b]
Usage: [code][code]func main() {}[/code][/code]
Result:
[code]func main() {}[/code]

[b]Horizontal Rule[/b]
Usage: [code][hr][/code]
Result:
[hr]
