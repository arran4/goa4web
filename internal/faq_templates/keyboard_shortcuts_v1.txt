Keyboard Shortcuts

What are the keyboard shortcuts?
===
# NOTE: When updating this file, please also update core/templates/site/keyboard_shortcuts.gohtml

[b]Global[/b]
* [b]Ctrl[/b] + [b]?[/b] : Show this help
* [b]Alt[/b] + [b]Home[/b] : Go to Home

[b]Forum[/b]
* [b]j[/b] / [b]k[/b] : Next/Prev Comment
* [b]q[/b] : Quote Selected Text

[b]Editor[/b]
* [b]Ctrl[/b] + [b]Enter[/b] : Submit Post
