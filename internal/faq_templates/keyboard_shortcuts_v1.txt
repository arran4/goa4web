Common keyboard shortcuts for navigation and editing.
===
What are the keyboard shortcuts?
===
Here are the available keyboard shortcuts:

[b Global]
* [b Ctrl] + [b ?] : Show this help
* [b Alt] + [b Home] : Go to Home

[b Forum]
* [b j] / [b k] : Next/Prev Comment
* [b q] : Quote Selected Text

[b Editor]
* [b Ctrl] + [b Enter] : Submit Post
